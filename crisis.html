Project Overview

For my second major project, I analyzed Character.AI’s crisis communication surrounding user safety concerns and created both a written crisis report and an infographic. The report required me to evaluate how the company communicated responsibility, risk, and safety to a public audience. I focused on making a clear argument about where the company’s messaging was effective and where it fell short. The infographic translated the same issue into a more visual and accessible format by using a timeline, statistics, and a cycle diagram to explain user attachment to AI companions. Together, these deliverables show my ability to communicate complex ethical and technical issues through both written and visual modes.

Revision and Process

One major revision I made was turning the infographic from a text-heavy visual into a more organized, multimodal asset. I used clearer sections, stronger visual hierarchy, and more intentional graphics so the audience could understand the issue quickly without feeling overwhelmed.
