About Me

I am a Computer Science student at Georgia Tech interested in software engineering, artificial intelligence, and technical problem-solving. Across my academic, professional, and project experiences, I have learned how important it is to communicate technical information clearly for different audiences. Whether I am building software, analyzing data, creating technical documentation, or presenting project findings, I try to focus on making information useful, accessible, and easy to understand.

This portfolio highlights my growth as a technical communicator through projects from LMC 3403 and work beyond the classroom. My projects show how I use writing, design, visual communication, and revision to explain complex ideas more clearly. They also reflect my larger professional goals as a future software engineer: to build thoughtful technical solutions while communicating effectively with both technical and non-technical audiences.
